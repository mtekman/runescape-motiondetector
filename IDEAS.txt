Mine clicker based on two things:

	* Mo-Detect on a given rock locus:
		Deps:
		* Size of locus
		* Position
		Ops:
		* Twinkle of rock (within a known region) --> Queue for ready
		* Colour of rock --> Dead, check another

	* Clicking:
		* Selects regions to monitor (for every movement update)
		* Time min,max -- update intervals for clicker
